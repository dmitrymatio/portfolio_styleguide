Logos

Logo Mark

The logo mark is essentially an avatar. I wanted to emphasize on my person and my skillset. The avatar is a simple auto generated trace of my face features combined with an existing illustration of a person. The added laptop is my 'weapon of choice' and immediatly communicates a realtion to computers. The added initials make it more personal to me and maintains continuity with the other versions of the logo.

Logo Type

The logo type is variations of my name at different lengths since my name is a bit long. Additional versions with no fill adds style options over accent colours. I wanted to convey a confident image with solid foundations so I chose Azo Sans Uber Regular as the font and stacked first and last name.

Don't

Colours

From left to right:

Sizzling Red will be used for call to action and hover effects
Orange Peel will be used for highlights and contrast with the darker colors
Dark Cornflower Blue will be used for primary buttons and contrast with the lighter backgrounds
Davys grey will be used for footers, elevation blocks, and contrast as a darker background; Also text on light backgrounds
Platinum will be the primary main content background and the primary option for card backgrounds also text on dark background

These are some of my favourite colour combinations, and I thought it would be ideal for my personnal portfolio.

Typography

This font is used everywhere else. Primarily used for body copy.
